HELP University

Assistant Manager, Corporate Communications

HELP University is one of Malaysia's top private universities with more than 30 years of footprints in Malaysia's education industry, possessing strong industry linkage and partnerships with foreign institutions.

Here, I worked as an Assistant Manager in the Corporate Communications Department while seconding to the A&P Division within the Customer Marketing Department.

Key Tasks

  • Content development

    Generated print and digital content for Advertising, Promotion and Marketing materials for 19 departments across multiple tertiary institutions; i.e. newspaper advertorials, advertisements, prospectus, brochures, flyers, radio scripts, storyboards and scripts for cinema advertisements, websites, social and digital media platforms, EDMs, etc.

  • Digital media

    Directed and monitored the organization's various social media platforms, which included preparing the social media schedule, conceptualizing and developing content (text and video) for the daily feed, designing visuals and monitoring digital campaign results. Developed monthly e-newsletters, EDMs for promotion and announcements, etc. Liaise with the numerous academic and administrative departments, which included producing write-ups and photographs for academic, marketing and corporate events.

  • Media liaison

    Served as a liaison officer to external agencies to disseminate content to different channels, including media, education portals, magazines, etc. Explore partnerships with new agencies (digital and media) to drive marketing objectives and develop content more efficiently. Liased with the media, handled media enquiries and assisted with the planning and execution of the monthly media schedule

  • Management

    Delegated work and assignments, and provided advice and supervision to junior staff, in regards to proofreading and content development.

Brands: HELP University, HELP College of Art and Technology, HELP Academy and ELM Graduate School.
